Culture & History

Subtitle: Timeless heritage meets modern vitality

Shanghai blends ancient Chinese history with vibrant international culture. Explore iconic landmarks, traditional gardens, historic architecture and local heritage sites while receiving medical care. Experience authentic traditions, art and customs in one of the world’s most fascinating cities — a perfect balance of healing and discovery.

Food & Local Life

Subtitle: Taste authentic flavors, live like a local

Experience Shanghai’s rich food culture and daily local life during your recovery. From delicate regional cuisine to healthy dietary choices tailored for wellness, you can enjoy fresh, balanced meals that suit your taste and recovery needs. Stroll through local streets, experience authentic daily life, and feel the warm, relaxed vibe of the city — making your medical trip comfortable and full of life.
